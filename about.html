In 2021 I wanted to learn to make my own clothes. This was partly motivated by the fact that I discovered neurodiversity. Expressing my individuality means having clothes that are fully tailored to my needs and specific personal style. Learning to sew on my own seemed like almost impossible task so I have set up this website to share information and encourage a sewing community in Leipzig.

This website is written and fully coded by myself using HTML, CSS & JavaScript. I set up and manage the Leipzig Wardrobe Youtube channel and filmed all the videos posted there. I also set up and manage the Leipzig Wardobe Instagram account.

A multipotentialite who was previously a Business English teacher in Barcelona for 6 years. Curently upskilling for a future proof career in coding.

It is said that sewing needs to skip a generation to experience a revival. My grandma was a sewing machine sales woman travelling up and down the UK to department stores. I didn't even know what a sewing machine was until last year! Now I manage 18 at the MakerSpace textile department!
