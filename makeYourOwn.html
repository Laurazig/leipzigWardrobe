What machine to buy

buying second hand

The sewing repair engineer Lehmann swears by the Veritas 80/1426 for household sewing machines because there are spare parts still available.

Make sure you can get a manual for the machine. I have had extreme difficulty sourcing manuals for the 18 old machines at MakerSpace in Gohlis.
