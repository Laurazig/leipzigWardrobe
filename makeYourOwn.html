buying second hand

The sewing repair engineer Lehmann swears by the Veritas 8014/26 for household sewing machines because there are spare parts still available.

Make sure you can get a manual for the machine. I have had extreme difficulty sourcing them

new

I read on a repair workshop website that they do not repair "Lidl and Aldi brands and cheap imports, because the cost of repair exceeds the cost of the machine." So buy the best quality you can.

If you are lucky enough to have a budget of 650€, buy the PFAFF Select 3.2.
